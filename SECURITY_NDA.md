## 1. Purpose
This Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) governs the disclosure of potential security vulnerabilities or sensitive information (“Confidential Information”) by the Researcher in The Bazaar repository or associated systems. It ensures responsible disclosure and protection of the Company’s assets.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:
- Security vulnerabilities, bugs, or exploits in code, infrastructure, or configuration
- API keys, service credentials, or access tokens
- Payment integrations (Stripe, M-Pesa)
- Backend services (Supabase, Fastify/Next.js)
- CI/CD pipelines, environment variables, and deployment configurations
- Documentation and internal technical data

---

## 3. Obligations of the Researcher
The Researcher agrees to:
1. Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential and not share with third parties.
2. Use Confidential Information only to report security vulnerabilities to the Company.
3. Not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the issue.
4. Not publicly disclose vulnerabilities until the Company confirms resolution.
5. Delete or return all Confidential Information on request.

---

## 4. Reporting
- Submit reports via **lumiotech30@gmail.com** or **GitHub Security Advisories**.  
- Include: description, reproduction steps, and potential impact.  
- The Company will acknowledge receipt and provide updates until resolved.

---

## 5. Rights
This NDA does **not grant any intellectual property rights** to the Researcher, except for the limited right to verify and report security issues.

---

## 6. Legal & Ethical Compliance
- Researcher must conduct testing **ethically and legally**.  
- The Company will not take legal action for **good-faith reporting** within this NDA’s scope.  
- Exploitation outside the agreed scope may result in legal consequences.

---

## 7. Duration
This NDA remains in effect from the first vulnerability report until all Confidential Information is resolved or publicly disclosed.

---

## 8. Governing Law
This NDA is governed by the laws of **Kenya**, unless otherwise agreed.

---

## 9. Acceptance
By submitting a vulnerability report, the Researcher agrees to the terms of this NDA.
